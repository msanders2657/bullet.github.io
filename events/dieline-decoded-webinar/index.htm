Dieline Decoded Webinar: Mini Eggs with Jamie Gandee

Event Date:

10 March 2022

Event Location: https://thedieline.com/blog/2022/3/15/dieline-decoded-cadbury-mini-eggs-with-bulletproof?

More info

Join our Creative Director Jamie Gandee as he decodes the design process behind Cadbury Mini Eggs. Learn how we redesigned the "Icon of Easter" by capturing the intrinsic feeling of happy Easter memories in the brand's new visual identity. Tune in for a revealing behind-the-design live case study, followed by Q&A with Jamie.
